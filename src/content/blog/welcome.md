## A Complete Beauty Companion

We're excited to introduce Chitti — a personal AI beauty companion that brings together dedicated specialists, deep skin analysis, smart routines, and progress tracking in one private, emotionally safe app.

Beauty advice has always been fragmented. You go to one place for skincare tips, another for haircare, and yet another for makeup. None of them know your products, your skin type, or what you've already tried. Chitti changes that.

## What Makes Chitti Different

**AI Specialists that know you.** Chat with dedicated skincare, haircare, and makeup experts. Every conversation is seeded with your skin analysis, product inventory, and routine history — so advice is personal from message one.

**Deep Skin Analysis.** Multi-angle photo capture analyzes 13 skin attributes — from hydration and texture to dark spots and pore size. Results feed directly into every specialist conversation.

**Product Tracking without forms.** Mention products naturally in conversation, and Chitti automatically adds them to your inventory. No barcode scanning, no manual entry.

**Smart Routines & Trials.** Build AM/PM skincare routines with proper step ordering. Test new products with structured 28-day trials and guided check-ins — like having a dermatologist on speed dial.

**Vibe Check.** Your smart mirror. Open the camera, and get instant AI feedback in under 5 seconds — with spatial labels overlaid right where they matter.

**Progress Tracking.** Capture progress photos over time and watch your skin transform. Before/after comparisons with AI trend analysis show what's working.

**Privacy first.** Your photos are processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. What you share with Chitti stays between you and Chitti.
